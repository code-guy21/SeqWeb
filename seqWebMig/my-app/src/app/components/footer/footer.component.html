What is Seq?

An electronic audio sequencer with Bluetooth enabled data transfer. The purpose of an audio sequencer is to allow musicians and hobbyists to develop musical patterns quickly. The goal is this project is to provide a more cost effective, portable and simple alternative to other audio sequencers in the market.
